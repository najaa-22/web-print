MES COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

MES College of Engineering, Kuttippuram, is a self-financing engineering college.
The college offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs including B.Tech, B.Arch, M.Tech, MCA, MBA, and Ph.D., and is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University .
